ABOUT ME


My name is Danil, I live in Ukraine and I am a Launchpadder. In 2015 I saw the Nev's cover to "First Of The Year". On that moment I was playing On iPad using Drum Pads 24 app. I just inloved in launchpad and I wanted to buy it. My first one was a Launchpad S. I was really happy when I got it. I didn't find any russian/ukrainian tutorials on how to use Ableton, how to set-up launchpad and other things, so I learned all about that for myself. I was making a lot of mistakes... Then I found RAMSES's channel, and when I was known about that he is russian - I was happy! Via him I got new friends... BEST friends: Denking and ABSOLUTE. It's sad that ABSOLUTE has left the launchpad community, but still. It's almost 2 years of our friendship with Denking. And now I know almost everybody in Launchpad community, and I found new best friends: T4sh, mat1jaczyyy, NeFFeN, avrdude, Gohma and others.
I love every my subscriber, every my friend, I am happy that I found that wonderful people as you. Thank you for your support.
